Overview

At least 25,108 deaths occurred in truck accidents during 2018. A truck's speed rarely goes beyond 50-60 km per hour, but they account for a high number of road fatalities every year. One of the primary reason is sleep , most of accidents are caused because the driver slept while driving.

Features

This WebApp continuosly tracks the eye and checks if the eyes are closed or not. If the CNN found that the user is sleeping then it will send an alert.
